The System

RTMS is an open-minded multi-martial art system built around one concept: the student is self-powered, not conforming to a master's style.

The system combines Modern American Ninjitsu and MMA to create Ninja-MMA, a practical martial science that teaches movement, not just moves. The system changes daily as knowledge is applied and refined. Every session builds on a complete framework, not a collection of techniques.

RTMS covers 5 Ranking Levels, from beginner to instructor status. Each level contains 9 areas of study. Every area builds on the ones before it. The goal is not a belt. The goal is capability.

9 Areas

Each of the 5 ranking levels covers these 9 areas of study.

  • 1
    Stances

    The foundation of all movement. 10 stances unlock 100+ techniques.

  • 2
    Rolls

    Ground movement, breakfalls, and evasion — falling safely is a skill, not an accident.

  • 3
    Falls

    Controlled descent. Land on your feet, your side, or your back without injury.

  • 4
    Strikes

    Hand strikes, elbow techniques, and upper-body offense and defense.

  • 5
    Kicks

    Lower-body techniques across multiple angles and heights.

  • 6
    Body Movement

    Evasion, footwork, and the spatial awareness that separates trained martial artists from fighters.

  • 7
    Knowledge (Mind)

    The mental component of RTMS. Winjitsu principles applied directly to the martial arts context.

  • 8
    Combat

    Applied sparring, scenario training, and real-world application of all previous areas.

  • 9
    Weapons

    Traditional and modern weapons training integrated into the full system.

PSC

The Personal Security Course is Rick's realistic self-defense program. It is integrated into every in-depth RTMS program.

PSC operates in this order: Mind, Body, Technique. Psychological strategy first. Physical skills second. Scenario training third. In a real situation, the person who panics loses before the first technique is thrown. PSC trains the mental state before it trains the body.

The course is designed equally for men and women. It does not assume prior experience and does not rely on size or strength. It relies on awareness, preparation, and trained instinct.

Mini-Martial Art

Rick's simplified entry system for beginners and corporate training. 10 stances. 100+ techniques.

The Mini-Martial Art is a scalable introduction to the full RTMS system. It uses 10 stances as the foundation. Each stance unlocks multiple techniques. The same 10 stances work from beginner to advanced level — the depth changes, not the structure.

Paired with Ninja Yoga (stretching and isometrics integrated with martial art positions), it creates a complete introductory program used in corporate training, beginner classes, and group workshops. It includes: martial art stances, cardio bursts, Ninja Yoga stretching, breathing techniques, and self-defense applications.

Fitness

RTMS training covers 5 fitness areas: Strength, Agility, Flexibility, Endurance, and Cardio.

The NinjaGym training methodology (the fitness backbone of all RTMS camps and programs) uses a 2-Minute Ninja Workout as its base HIIT format: 1 minute of high intensity followed by 1 minute of rest or warm-up. From this foundation, full training sessions are built to develop all 5 fitness areas simultaneously.

Physical training is always paired with mental training. Every physical skill in RTMS has a corresponding mental framework from Winjitsu. That is what makes it a martial science rather than just a martial art.
