Who

I'm Rick Tew. Martial arts instructor, peak performance coach, and founder. I created Winjitsu, RTMS, and NinjaGym. I live globally and teach people how to take charge of their lives.

I started teaching martial arts at 16. By 19, I had opened my first dojo. At 21, I moved to Europe to develop RTMS and spent over 6 years living and teaching across Holland, Rotterdam, Delft, and the surrounding areas. From there I expanded across Spain, California, Latin America, the Philippines, and Thailand.

My coaching certification comes from the Robbins-Madanes Training Center. I am a Tony Robbins Certified Strategic Intervention Coach. My speaking clients include HP, TU Delft, the University of Rotterdam, Dutch Art and Design, and Humaniversity. I have been featured in Black Belt Magazine, KO Karate, Fighting Spirit Zendokan, Fighting Sports, and International Lifestyle Magazines.

What

I teach three interconnected systems: RTMS (the physical martial science), Winjitsu (the mental martial art), and NinjaGym (the fitness methodology).

RTMS is a complete multi-martial art system with 5 ranking levels covering Modern American Ninjitsu, Ninja-MMA, and self-defense. Winjitsu is the mental counterpart: a 5-book system plus workshop for overcoming fear, building confidence, and taking action. NinjaGym is the physical training environment I built from the ground up, combining obstacle training, parkour, martial arts, and kids programs.

Everything I build comes from one principle: the best training addresses mind, body, and spirit together. Not separately. Not in sequence. Together.

When

The foundation started early.

Age 8: ran away to California. Boy Scouts gave me the framework for goals, leadership, and the outdoors. Age 14: fell in love with ninjitsu. Age 15: on television for martial arts skills. Age 16-17: started teaching. Age 19: opened first dojo. Age 21: moved to Europe to develop RTMS into a full system.

Over the following 30+ years, I built martialartsupply.com into a successful business, developed the Winjitsu series, launched NinjaGym in Thailand, and have been training students from every corner of the world.

Where

I live globally. Indiana was the origin. The world is the address.

I have lived and taught in Holland (Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Delft, Naarden), Spain, California, Thailand, Latin America, and the Philippines. My programs run in Samui, Thailand and near Yosemite, California. Students come from the US, Canada, Holland, Australia, Mexico, and beyond.

Why

"Just because you have a job doesn't mean you have a life."

Most people go through the motions. They have routines but not results. They have activity but not direction. I teach people to take ownership of the one life they have and build it with the same discipline a martial artist brings to the mat.

The word Ninja means to persevere. To endure. To survive. My goal is to help people apply that mindset to every part of life, not just the dojo.

How

Through camps, tours, coaching, speaking, and the systems I've built over three decades.

Live-in training camps in Thailand and California. BLAST travel and training tours. The Winjitsu book series. RTMS ranked training. NinjaGym programs for kids and adults. Speaking engagements for companies and universities. Strategic Intervention coaching for individuals.

Every format leads to the same destination: a person who is in charge of their own life.
