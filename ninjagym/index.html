The Gym

NinjaGym was founded and created by Rick Tew. It is both a physical training facility and the fitness methodology behind all of Rick's programs.

The gym is located in Samui, Thailand, on Ring Road, inside the Big C mall on the ground floor near the entrance (near Starbucks). It offers ninja training, obstacle courses, climbing walls, parkour, martial arts, and dedicated kids programs.

NinjaGym also operates as the wellness and fitness brand across all of Rick's camps and tours. The NinjaGym training methodology (bodyweight, obstacle-based, HIIT-driven) is the physical spine of the RTMS program.

Training Areas

NinjaGym training covers 5 fitness areas and is built around functional, bodyweight-first movement.

  • Strength

    Functional bodyweight and resistance training

  • Agility

    Speed, reaction, and directional change

  • Flexibility

    Ninja Yoga, stretching, and mobility

  • Endurance

    Sustained output and mental toughness

  • Cardio

    HIIT, 2-Minute Ninja Workout format

Kids Programs

NinjaGym's kids programs combine physical training, obstacle courses, and the Winjitsu mindset for younger students.

The goal is simple: give kids a space where they move, challenge themselves, build confidence, and have a blast doing it. No prior experience. No age restriction within reason. Drop in any day.

5-hour day camps run daily. Drop in before 3pm. Ideal for families visiting Samui or kids looking for an active alternative to the beach.
