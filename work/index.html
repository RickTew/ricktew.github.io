The Approach

Everything gets built because it should exist, not because there's a market slide deck that says so.

The vocabulary app exists because students needed one and nothing else did the job right. The recall app exists because Rick built a curriculum and needed a way to deliver it digitally. The games exist because the game ideas were too good to leave in a notebook.

Most of the apps connect back to the same systems: Winjitsu for the mental side, RTMS for the physical side, NinjaGym for the fitness side. The goal is to give people access to the full system in whatever format works best for them.
