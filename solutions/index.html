Give a BLAST

For every paid event Rick is hired for, one person whose life is not a BLAST attends a program free.

In the martial arts there is a training concept called give and take. Rick has extended this outside the dojo. For every paid speaking event or program, Rick reinvests back into the community and invites one person (identified through local charity organizations) to attend free of charge.
