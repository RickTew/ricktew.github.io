What It Is

Winjitsu is Rick Tew's own system for mental training and personal success. It is the mental counterpart to his physical martial arts system.

The word "Winjitsu" combines two ideas: WIN, meaning to succeed, and JITSU, meaning true art or true way. Together: the true art of winning.

In the physical martial arts, the opponent is outside you. In Winjitsu, the opponent is the negative Ninja inside: self-doubt, fear, negativity, stress, laziness, and loss. These are the real battles most people face every day. Winjitsu was built to help you fight them and win.

The mind-body connection is not a metaphor here. In today's world, the mental battles come before the physical ones. Winjitsu develops the mental skills first so the physical ones have a foundation to stand on.

The Series

Five Books. Five Fingers. One Fist.

The Winjitsu series is a 5-book system. Each book is one finger of the hand. Together they form a fist (the Warrior: break through) and a grip (the Cultivator: hang on and endure). Read them in order, reference them daily.

Book 1 — ACE Achieving Combined Excellence Taking Control of Your Attitude
Book 2 — MAK Motivation, Action, Knowledge Make it Happen
Book 3 — MBS Mind, Body, Spirit The Synergy for Success
Book 4 — NRG Neuro Reasons for Growth Internal Power for Achievement
Book 5 — CMT Creative Mental Training The Magic of Visualization

"Be a Black Belt in What You Do" — the tagline of the entire series. A black belt is not a destination. It is a mindset you carry into everything.

BLAST Workshop

The BLAST workshop is the live experience of the Winjitsu system in a single session.

BLAST stands for: Breathing, Laughing, Adventuring, Stretching, Thinking. The workshop is delivered in edutainment format, combining comedy, martial arts demonstrations, and Winjitsu principles. It works for corporate events, universities, and individuals.

The workshop covers: using your strengths, leading with your talents, organizing your motivation, taking action, balancing your life, building constant energy, overcoming limitations, crushing fear, building confidence, and using visualization.

The GIVE A BLAST program is built into every paid engagement. For every event Rick is hired for, one person whose life is not a BLAST attends free through local charity organizations.
