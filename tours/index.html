What Is BLAST

BLAST stands for: Breathing, Laughing, Adventuring, Stretching, Thinking. It is both the tour format and the training philosophy behind it.

BLAST tours are 2-week martial arts travel adventures for groups of like-minded people. You train daily with Rick. You explore the location together. You build real connections. The HI (Human Interaction) philosophy is at the center of every tour: put people together, remove distractions, let real experience happen.

Disconnect to Reconnect. That is not a slogan. It is the design principle. When you remove yourself from your normal environment, you can see yourself clearly. That is where change happens.

What You Do

Every day is a mix of training and exploration.

  • ◈
    Daily Martial Arts Training

    Morning and evening sessions with Rick. RTMS fundamentals, Ninja-MMA, self-defense, and Winjitsu principles woven through every class.

  • ◈
    Outdoor Adventure

    Hikes, national parks, rock formations, island training, and outdoor challenges tailored to the location. The landscape is part of the curriculum.

  • ◈
    Group Dynamics

    You train alongside people who are serious about growth. The group becomes a support system that extends beyond the tour.

  • ◈
    Winjitsu Sessions

    Mental training integrated throughout the two weeks. Not a lecture. A live workshop that runs in parallel with the physical training.
