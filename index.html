About

I'm a martial arts instructor, peak performance coach, and builder. I work globally and run NinjaGym in Thailand.

I've spent decades teaching that martial arts is about far more than fighting. Traditional martial arts training delivers exactly what research shows produces happiness: intense focus, real challenge, clear objectives, and immediate feedback. That formula works in the dojo. It works in a career. It works in building a business or shipping a game.

I developed Winjitsu, a mental martial art and system for success, and wrote a five-book series called "Be a Black Belt in What You Do" to take those principles everywhere. Your challenges today are more mental than physical. Your mental skills will take you further than your physical ones. That is what Winjitsu is built around.

I coach in 5's. Who, What, When, Where, Why. Then How. Everything I build follows that structure. I noticed my own website didn't follow it. This one does.

On the side (or maybe it's the main thing now) I build apps and games. Things I wanted to exist, so I made them.

Martial Arts Therapy

The unique ability to combine the mind and the martial arts makes Rick an effective Martial Arts Therapist.

  • ◈
    Overcome traumatic events

    Structured physical challenge creates a path through, not around, difficult experiences.

  • ◈
    Build self-confidence

    Earned through repetition, honest feedback, and progressive challenge, not empty praise.

  • ◈
    Pain and fear management

    Learning to move through discomfort in a controlled environment transfers to every area of life.

  • ◈
    Release post-traumatic stress

    Physical training provides a proven, non-clinical outlet for the nervous system to reset.

  • ◈
    Redirect aggression

    Channeling energy into disciplined practice is one of the oldest and most effective tools we have.
