About

I'm a martial arts instructor, peak performance coach, and builder — based in Samui, Thailand, where I run NinjaGym.

I've spent decades teaching that martial arts isn't about fighting — it's a system for life. I developed Winjitsu, a mental martial art, and wrote a five-book series called "Be a Black Belt in What You Do" to take those principles everywhere: your career, your habits, your creative work.

I coach in 5's. Who, What, When, Where, Why — then How. Everything I build follows that structure, because focused challenges with clear objectives and immediate feedback is where real growth happens. I noticed my own website didn't follow it. This one does.

On the side — or maybe it's the main thing now — I build apps and games. Things I wanted to exist, so I made them.
