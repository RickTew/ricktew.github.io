The Program

The Camp of Martial Science (CMS) is a live-in multi-martial arts ninja training program. It started near Yosemite National Park and now runs in Thailand and California.

This is not a weekend seminar. This is full immersion. You live with the program. You train daily. You disconnect from your regular environment so you can reconnect with what matters. HI (Human Interaction) is the philosophy behind every session: real conversation, real challenge, real growth with real people.

No prior experience is required. What is required: commitment. If you are serious about improving your life, there is a path forward here.

5X Training

Every camp covers five interconnected training tracks running simultaneously.

  • 1
    Success Skills

    Winjitsu coaching and life strategies. Goal setting, visualization, overcoming fear, building confidence. The mental training that makes the physical training stick.

  • 2
    Physical Skills

    HIIT, strength, agility, flexibility, endurance, and cardio. NinjaGym training methodology applied in a live training environment.

  • 3
    Adventure Skills

    Outdoor training, rock climbing, hiking, camping, road trips, and exploration. The outdoor component is not optional. Adventure is part of the training.

  • 4
    Self-Defense Skills

    The PSC (Personal Security Course). Mind first, then body, then technique. Realistic self-defense for men and women with no prior experience required.

  • 5
    Martial Arts Skills

    Ninja-MMA using the complete RTMS 5-level ranked system. You train the full system from day one, at your current level.
