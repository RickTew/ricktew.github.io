## SYSTEMS AND PROGRAMS

### RTMS — Rick Tew's Martial Science
- Not a sport or style — a Martial Science and way of life
- Open-minded multi-martial art system: Modern American Ninjitsu + MMA
- 5 Ranking Levels to Instructor status
- 9 areas per level: Stances, Rolls, Falls, Strikes, Kicks, Body Movement, Knowledge (mind), Combat, Weapons
- Student is self-powered — not conforming to a master's style
- System changes daily — teaches movement, not just moves

### Winjitsu — The Mental Martial Art
- Rick's own system for mental training and personal success
- "Win" (to succeed) + "Jitsu" (true art/way) = True Art of Winning
- Life is an obstacle course; Winjitsu teaches you to navigate it
- Mind-Body connection applied to modern challenges (mental > physical in today's world)
- Used to defeat "negative Ninja" (self-doubt, fear, negativity, stress, laziness)

**What Winjitsu teaches:**
- Self-confidence, self-esteem, self-discipline, self-power, self-control, self-image
- Overcoming fear, doubt, negativity, stress, laziness, loss
- Goal setting, visualization, memory, mental flexibility

**The BLAST Workshop** — summarizes Winjitsu system; teaches:
- Be and feel fulfilled by focusing on dreams
- Be and Do more by taking action
